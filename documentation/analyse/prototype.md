## üì± 1. Prototyping Goals

*   **Visualize the Solutions:** Transform abstract concepts from the 'Ideate' phase into interactive or static screens.
*   **Test Usability Quickly:** Create low-fidelity (wireframes) to high-fidelity (mockups) designs to get early feedback before development.
*   **Focus on the "Double-Entry" Fix:** Prioritize the screens where data is inputted (Teachers) and verified (Administration).

---

## üñ•Ô∏è 2. Key Interfaces to Prototype

### **A. Teacher's Input Interface (Mobile/Web)**
*   **Goal:** Allow teachers to record attendance in under 2 minutes.
*   **Key Elements:**
    *   Class list with clear "Present" / "Absent" / "Late" toggle buttons.
    *   Quick "Select All as Present" button to save time.
    *   Simple submit button to instantly sync data.

### **B. Administration's Verification Dashboard (The Hub)**
*   **Goal:** Provide Madam Hannane with a quick, color-coded overview of the entire school's attendance status.
*   **Key Elements:**
    *   High-level metrics (e.g., Total Present, Total Absent, Pending Justifications).
    *   List of absent students highlighting unverified or unexcused absences.
    *   Action shortcuts to "Approve" or "Reject" submitted medical notes.
    *   Export button for daily reports.

### **C. Student/Parent Transparency Portal**
*   **Goal:** Allow students to check their absence record and justification status without visiting the office.
*   **Key Elements:**
    *   Simple summary of total absences.
    *   List of submitted justifications with their current status (Pending, Approved, Rejected).
    *   A clean, easy-to-use "Upload Document" button for new medical notes.
