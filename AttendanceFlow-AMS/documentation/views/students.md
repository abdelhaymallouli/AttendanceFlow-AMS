# Student Views Documentation

The Student views focus on transparency and self-management of attendance records.

## 📄 `student/dashboard.blade.php`
- **Role**: Personal attendance summary.
- **Visuals**:
  - **Large Circular Gauge**: Shows the current attendance rate.
  - **Absence Log**: A list of recently missed sessions.
  - **Upcoming Schedule**: A preview of the next classes for their group.

## 📄 `student/justifications.blade.php`
- **Role**: Absence justification workspace.
- **Features**:
  - Simple form to upload text and documents explaining an absence.
  - History table showing the status of past justifications (Pending/Accepted/Rejected).
